Who can participate

A person with weight higher than what is considered normal for a given height and is considered overweight (BMI 27 kg/m2 or greater with at least one weight-related conditions (high blood pressure, type 2 diabetes, or high cholesterol) or and obese 30 kg/m2 or greater).

Prescription Weight Loss

There is “no magic pill” that will cure obesity, but there is a new type of medication that is a potential game-changer. Semaglutide, was FDA approved in 2021 to treat obesity and overweight with at least one medically related condition.

How it works

Is a weekly injection administered under the skin and works by mimicking a hormone that targets areas of the brain and delays gastric emptying which can reduce appetite, improve control of eating and decrease food cravings.

Proven results

Semaglutide may not work for everyone, but when it’s successful, it can help someone shed 10 - 15% of their body weight. Studies have shown that losing weight can improve your health as well as decrease your risks, diabetes heart disease and even death.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions


How do I book an appointment?

To make an appointment, please use the contact page to schedule your online appointment. You will be contacted by staff during regular hours to confirm the appointment.

Do I need a referral?

While it is ideal to have a referral from another provider, it is not necessary. Prescreening forms are included in the request for the appointment to assist in meeting your needs.

How do I access my portal?

Once an appointment date has been confirmed, you will receive an email with portal access instructions to complete prior to initial evaluation.

What is your cancellation policy?

In the event that you need to reschedule or cancel your appointment, we ask that you give us 48 hours' notice. All late cancellations are subject to a fee.

Do you take insurance?

We're sorry, but we don't accept insurance as payment. We place the utmost importance on the quality & efficiency of our services. Our prices are reflective of this in our care.

Can I get refills?

During the course of treatment, it is your responsibility to manage appointments in a timely manner to avoiding running out of medication. Refills for medications will NOT be provided without a regularly scheduled appointment.
